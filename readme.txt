== Description ==
## Crypto Voucher Widget
---

Is an innovative no-code solution which allows your users to buy Crypto Vouchers on your website directly.
Whole process is completely hosted by us and does not require any attention from your side.
We are taking full responsibility for the transactions,
so you donâ€™t need to worry about chargebacks while keeping the best conversion rate in the market.

Crypto Voucher is a gift card that you can redeem into various cryptocurrencies such as
Bitcoin, Litecoin, DogeCoin, Etherum, USDC, BNB, MATIC.
You can keep your funds in our internal wallet or send them directly to your external wallet.
We offer more than 200+ payments including Credit cards and PayPal available for 160+ countries worldwide.

In order for our widget to work properly, it is necessary to connect to our services, so
your data will be processed by our api.

This plugin is only a wrapper for javascript library published here:
https://www.npmjs.com/package/@crypto-voucher/widget

For the widget to work properly on your website,
it is necessary to create an account at [Crypto Voucher](https://www.cryptovoucher.io/ "Crypto Voucher")
